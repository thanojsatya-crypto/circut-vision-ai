## 1. CORE FORMULAS & MATHEMATICS
1. Z = ω²L/C  
2. Q = ω/(2πRC)  
3. Impedance Matching Z₀ = √(L/C)  
4. Resonance Frequency ω₀ = 1/√(LC)  
5. Small Signal Approximation Z ≈ Z₀  
6. Thermal Stability Threshold T₀  

## 2. DESIGN ORDER OF OPERATIONS
1. Calculate Z₀ first  
2. Determine component values sequentially  
3. Test parameters iteratively  

## 3. STANDARD VALUES & PARAMETERS
12V supply, 100kΩ resistors, 1MΩ capacitors, 2N4148 ICs  

## 4. PRACTICAL COMPONENT SELECTION RULES
Use electrolytic capacitors for high capacitance, ceramic for stability, 1/4W resistors for low power  

## 5. COMMON MISTAKES & TROUBLESHOOTING
1. Incorrect frequency calculation  
2. Wrong component sizing  
3. Misreading datasheets  
4. Overlooking thermal limits  
5. Incorrect impedance matching  

## 6. VIVA & INTERVIEW TRAPS
1. Resonance frequency miscalculation  
2. Stability under load  
3. Signal attenuation errors  
4. Component failure points  
5. Frequency drift issues
